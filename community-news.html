Ministry and LeFA jointly put M300 000 for Likuena to beat Seychelles

By:LeFA Reporter

The Lesotho Football Association President Mr.Lijane Nthunya has announced a M300 000 incentive to the Senior National Team Likuena, to beat Seychelles in the upcoming back-to-back 2027 Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON)preliminary Round Fixtures.

Making the announcement in the company of the Lesotho Sports and Recreation Commission President Baba Malepane, during a press conference held at Bambatha Ts'ita Sports Arena, the LeFA chief revealed that his office has been working hand in hand with the LSRC boss in soucing funds from the Ministry to uplift the Likuena Camp ahead of its two games.

The LeFA President stated that the government of Lesotho through the Ministry of Tourism, Sports, Arts an Culture has put M150 000 as an incentive to Likuena should they advance to the group phase of the qualifiers while LeFA will also dig deep from its coffers to put another M150 000.

"We gave our Ministry a knock at their door through the support of LSRC and we are proud to to inform the football fraternity that the ministry has put forward M150 000 and we will also dig deep from our coffers as LeFA to put another another M150 000 to make it M300 000 as an incentive to Likuena to beat Seychelles,"LeFA President said.

He stated that Likuena should not be playing in the preliminary qualifiers of any competition and the Bob Mafoso coached side should win against the islanders by hook or crook.

Likuena's back-to-back fixtures against Seychelles will be played at Toyota Stadium in Bloemfontein on March 26th and 29th respectively.

The Sam Matekane foundation, Econet Telecom Lesotho supported by the Lesotho Premier League host the People's cup 4th Edition Tournament

By:Lesotho Premier League

The Sam Matekane Foundation in joint with Econet Telecom Lesotho, the Premier League Management Commitee and key stakeholdes are yet to host the People's Cup 4th Edition tournament at Bocheletsane, Mants'onyane on the 26thApril 2026.

Now on its 4th Edition the People's cup returns bigger and more exciting with a M1.1 million sponsorship, further cementing it place as one of the major tournaments in the mountain Kingdom.

The 2026 prize pool is structured as follows:

  • 1st prize:M400 000
  • 2nd prize:M200 000
  • 3rd prize:M100 000
  • 4th prize:M100 000
In addition the individual awards will be as follows:
  1. Player of the tournament M5000
  2. Top goal scorer M4000
  3. Goalkeeper of the tournament M2000
  4. Man of the Match (M1500 per Match) M4500
  5. Match official of the tournament M1000

img.Bantu FC-2024/25 People's cup Champions

Speaking at the press conference that was held at Bambatha Ts'ita Sports Arena on the 19th March, the PLMC Chairman Mr. Hlompho Maitin, thanked the sponsors of the tournament and praised their role in elevating the local games.

"The people's Cup has become one of the major tournaments in Lesotho and has been a platform for growing football as supporters are put at the center of action while also creating direct value for their teams," Chairman said.
